About

ScribbleScape is a tiny indie studio making beautiful experimental games. It was awarded UK Games Funding in 2020, was part of Elevate 2020 and then part of the Games London Accelerator in 2021.

ScribbleScape is run by Caroline, who was awarded a place as an Astra Fellow in 2023. She is currently working on her unannounced Astra project and alongside Queensguard on Shards Between Us.
